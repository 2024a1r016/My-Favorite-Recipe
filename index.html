Indian Maggie

Indian Maggi is a popular brand of instant noodles that has become a cultural phenomenon in India. Introduced in 1983 by Nestlé, it quickly gained popularity as a quick and convenient meal option, especially among urban households and young people.

Ingredients

  • Maggi Noodles
  • Vegetables
  • Oil
  • Spices
  • Maggi Tastemaker
  • Water: About 2 cups

Instructions

  1. Prepare the vegetables: Chop the onions, carrots, bell peppers, and any other vegetables you're using.
  2. Sauté: Heat oil in a pan and sauté the chopped onions until translucent. Add other vegetables and cook for a couple of minutes.
  3. Add spices and tastemaker: Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and the Maggi tastemaker.
  4. Add water and noodles: Pour in the water and bring to a boil. Add the Maggi noodles and cook until they are tender and the water is absorbed.
  5. Garnish and serve: Garnish with coriander leaves or other preferred toppings and serve hot.
